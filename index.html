How to Use Book Scanner

1. Scan Books: Point your camera at any book barcode to scan it instantly.
2. Get AI Analysis: Receive smart summaries, key insights, and recommendations.
3. Save to Library: Build your personal book collection with favorites and notes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of books can I scan?
A: You can scan any book with a barcode (ISBN). This includes most books from bookstores and libraries.
Q: Do I need internet connection?
A: Yes, you need internet to get AI analysis and book information.
Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes, your personal library is stored locally on your device. We only use book information for analysis.
